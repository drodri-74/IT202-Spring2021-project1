Search App
Description:
This is an app that focuses on searching "id" numbers of properties currently or historically owned and managed by the City of Chicago. The app uses a filtered id query that you will have to select in the Form tab. After you selected your option and click "search" the app will send you to the Data tab for your selected results.
You may also look at the Map tab so you could be able to identify the location in "Google maps".
